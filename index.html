About Me

I'm a Los Angeles based web developer with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, and the MEAN stack. I have a background in engineering, working at Marine Corps Systems Command in Quantico, VA for three years where I focused on technical writing, test planning/execution, and acting as a technical advisor for multiple project officers. In my off-time, I started to teach myself basic web technologies which revived the enthusiasm I had found for coding in college. Once I moved out to Los Angeles, I acted on that enthusiasm and started at General Assembly to build up my skills in order to become a professional web developer. I bring with me an engineer's mindset, including a history of problem solving, positive client interaction, and the ability to effectively communicate technical concepts to non-technical minded audiences.
